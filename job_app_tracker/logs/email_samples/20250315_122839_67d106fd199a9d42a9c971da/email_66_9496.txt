In this week's edition of Forbes Edge, we'll offer up advice on applying for a new position at the start of spring, dig into February's weak jobs report and more. But first, a look at how the workplace has shifted (and shifted again) in the time since the Covid-19 pandemic began five years ago. Do you remember where you were on March 11, 2020?  Five years ago this week, the World Health Organization declared Covid-19 a pandemic, sending workers home from the office and shuttering businesses worldwide.
