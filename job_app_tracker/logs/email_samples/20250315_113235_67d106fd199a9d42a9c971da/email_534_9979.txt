Improve sleep with a clean mattress
 
We spend almost a third of our lives in bed, but studies show that we don’t regularly clean our mattresses. Today is World Sleep Day. Celebrate by prioritizing getting a good night’s rest by following these tips for proper mattress cleaning with Dyson technology.
 
Mattress cleaning tips
 
Research shows that many people clean their bedrooms most often, but that doesn’t include the mattress. Mattresses may look clean but have microscopic dirt, dust, and allergens on them that can impact your sleep.
 
Clean the mattress surface
 
Remove all bedding. Attach the Fabric and mattress tool to your vacuum to remove dust, dirt, and allergens from the mattress surface.
