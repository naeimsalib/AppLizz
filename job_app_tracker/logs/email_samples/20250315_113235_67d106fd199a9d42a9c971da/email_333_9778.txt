Hello Naeim Salib,

ngrok allows you to create secure ingress to any app, device or service without spending hours learning arcane networking technologies. You can get started with a single command or a single line of code.

ngrok’s platform is built around 3 main capabilities that connect the world to your work. Those capabilities have many different options depending on what you’re building, so take a moment to review them.

1. Connect: You can establish ingress in a few ways, depending on what you’re trying to solve:
