You’ve probably heard Gary Vee yelling about going live the past few months. And if you’re a founder selling a high-ticket offer, I’m here to tell you, he’s absolutely right. Here's what is: Start going live on LinkedIn on VIDEO (click here to register for the 4-Day LinkedIn Masterclass if you want to start). That means when you go live, you’re instantly in the top 1% of visibility. Add to that the fact that 4 out of 5 LinkedIn users are decision-makers (aka, no middlemen slowing down your sales process), and it’s clear: LinkedIn Live is a massive untapped opportunity.
