Frequently Asked Questions

What is Applizz?

Applizz is a comprehensive job application tracking tool designed to help job seekers organize and monitor their job search process. It allows you to track applications, interview stages, and receive notifications about status changes. The app can also scan your emails to automatically detect job applications and updates.

How do I add a new job application?

To add a new job application manually:

  1. Navigate to your Dashboard
  2. Click the "Add Application" button
  3. Fill out the application form with details like company name, position, application date, etc.
  4. Click "Save" to add the application to your tracker
Alternatively, you can connect your email account and Applizz will automatically detect job applications from your emails.

How does the email integration work?

Applizz can connect to your email account (Gmail, Outlook, or Yahoo) to automatically scan for job-related emails. When it finds emails related to job applications or status updates, it creates suggestions that you can review and approve. Here's how it works:

  1. Go to Settings and connect your email account
  2. Click "Scan Emails" to search for job-related emails
  3. Review suggestions in the "Email Suggestions" section
  4. Approve or reject each suggestion
You can configure whether Applizz requires your approval before making changes based on emails.

Is my email data secure?

Yes, Applizz takes your privacy and security seriously:

  • We use OAuth for Gmail and Outlook, which means we never store your actual password
  • For Yahoo Mail, we use app passwords which are specific to our application and can be revoked at any time
  • We only scan emails for job-related content and don't store the full content of your emails
  • You can disconnect your email account at any time from the Settings page
  • All data is stored securely in our database with proper encryption

How do I update the status of an application?

To update the status of an application:

  1. Go to your Dashboard
  2. Find the application you want to update
  3. Click the "Edit" button (pencil icon)
  4. Change the status in the dropdown menu
  5. Click "Save" to update the application
Alternatively, if you've connected your email, Applizz may automatically detect status changes from emails and suggest updates.

How do I connect my Yahoo Mail account?

To connect your Yahoo Mail account:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Click "Connect Yahoo Mail"
  3. You'll need to generate an app password from your Yahoo account:
    • Log in to your Yahoo account
    • Go to Account Security settings
    • Scroll to "App passwords"
    • Generate a new app password for "Applizz"
  4. Enter the 16-character app password in Applizz
  5. Click "Connect" to complete the process
If you encounter authentication issues, try generating a new app password and ensure you're copying it exactly as shown.

Can I export my application data?

Currently, Applizz doesn't have a built-in export feature. However, we're working on adding the ability to export your application data to CSV or PDF formats in a future update. This will allow you to keep backups of your job application history or share it with career coaches.

What should I do if email scanning isn't working?

If you're having trouble with email scanning:

  1. Check that your email account is properly connected in Settings
  2. For Yahoo Mail, try generating a new app password
  3. Make sure you have job-related emails in your inbox
  4. Try disconnecting and reconnecting your email account
  5. Check if your email provider has any security settings that might be blocking access
If you continue to experience issues, you can still manually add and update applications through the dashboard.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your Applizz account:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the page
  3. Click the "Delete Account" button
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted
Please note that account deletion is permanent and will remove all your data, including job applications, settings, and email connections.

Is Applizz free to use?

Yes, Applizz is currently free to use with all features included. We may introduce premium features in the future, but our core functionality will always remain free to help job seekers manage their application process effectively.
