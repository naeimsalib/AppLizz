Frequently Asked Questions

What is Job Application Tracker?

Job Application Tracker is a simple tool designed to help job seekers organize and monitor their job search process. It allows you to track applications, interview stages, and maintain a clear overview of your job search progress.

How do I add a new job application?

To add a new job application:

  1. Navigate to your Dashboard
  2. Click the "Add Application" button
  3. Fill out the application form with details like company name, position, application date, etc.
  4. Click "Save" to add the application to your tracker

How do I update the status of an application?

To update the status of an application:

  1. Go to your Dashboard
  2. Find the application you want to update
  3. Click the "Edit" button (pencil icon)
  4. Change the status in the dropdown menu
  5. Click "Save" to update the application

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take your privacy and security seriously:

  • Your password is securely hashed before being stored
  • All data is stored securely in our database
  • We use HTTPS to encrypt all data transmission
  • We only collect the information necessary for the application tracking functionality

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the page
  3. Click the "Delete Account" button
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted
Please note that account deletion is permanent and will remove all your data, including job applications and settings.

What are Reminders and how do I use them?

Reminders are a powerful feature that helps you stay on top of your job applications:

  • Access reminders by clicking the bell icon on any application
  • Create reminders for follow-ups, deadlines, or any important tasks
  • Set due dates and descriptions for each reminder
  • Mark reminders as completed when you've finished the task
  • View all upcoming reminders on your dashboard
Why use reminders?
  • Never miss important application deadlines
  • Stay organized with follow-up tasks
  • Track multiple applications efficiently
  • Improve your response time to potential employers

How do I use the Analytics features?

The Analytics dashboard provides valuable insights into your job search:

  • View application status distribution in the Status Chart
  • Track your application timeline and progress
  • Monitor key metrics like:
    • Total applications submitted
    • Applications in progress
    • Interview success rate
    • Offer statistics
Benefits of Analytics:
  • Identify successful application strategies
  • Optimize your job search approach
  • Track your progress over time
  • Make data-driven decisions in your job search

How do I switch between Light and Dark mode?

You can easily switch between Light and Dark mode:

  • Look for the theme toggle switch in the navigation bar
  • Click the switch to toggle between Light and Dark mode
  • Your preference will be saved automatically
  • The app defaults to Light mode for optimal readability
Benefits:
  • Reduce eye strain in low-light conditions
  • Customize your viewing experience
  • Save battery life on OLED displays
